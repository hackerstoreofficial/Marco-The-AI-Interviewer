Interview Guidelines

Before we begin, please carefully read and acknowledge the following guidelines. These rules ensure a fair and secure interview environment.

Camera Access Required

Your camera must remain active throughout the interview. Our AI proctoring system monitors your presence and focus.

Microphone Access Required

Your microphone will be used for voice responses. Speak clearly and naturally during the interview.

Full Screen Sharing Mandatory

You must share your entire screen, not just a tab or window. This prevents the use of external aids.

⚠️ Anti-Cheat Policy

  • Gaze Tracking: Looking away from the screen more than 5 times will auto-terminate the interview.
  • Tab Switching: Switching tabs or windows more than 2 times will auto-terminate the interview.
  • No External Aids: Using notes, search engines, or other resources is strictly prohibited.
  • Time Limit: The interview will last a maximum of 30 minutes.
